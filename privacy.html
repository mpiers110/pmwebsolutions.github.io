Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

At Sellr POS, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our merchants and their customers. This policy outlines how we handle data across our point-of-sale system and AI analytics engine.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information necessary to run your shop and provide AI-driven insights:

  • Merchant Information: Business name, contact details, and location.
  • Transaction Data: Sales records, payment types (M-Pesa, card, cash), and timestamps.
  • Inventory Data: Product lists, stock levels, and supplier information.
  • Marketing Data: Customer phone numbers (for WhatsApp automated marketing) and loyalty records.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to empower your business growth:

  • To process and sync transactions across devices.
  • To generate AI Sale Insights and restock alerts.
  • To automate WhatsApp marketing campaigns on your behalf.
  • To provide technical support and security monitoring.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure your shop data is safe. Data is stored securely in regional data centers to minimize latency and comply with local regulations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your shop's data to third parties. We only share data with service providers necessary to operate Sellr (e.g., cloud hosting, payment gateways like Safaricom for M-Pesa STK Push).

5. Your Rights

You own your data. You can export your sales records, inventory lists, and customer data at any time through the Sellr Dashboard.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@sellr.co.ke.
